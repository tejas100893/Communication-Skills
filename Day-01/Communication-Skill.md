

*** Communication Skills ***

1. Tell me about yourself

“I have around 6 years of experience in Production Support within the banking domain. My current role involves application support, incident management, deployment activities, troubleshooting production issues, server-related operations, and coordination with multiple internal teams to maintain service availability.

I also handle critical incidents, support team activities, and participate in change and maintenance operations. Working in banking has helped me build experience in high-pressure environments where availability and quick resolution are important.

Alongside my current responsibilities, I’m actively upgrading my skills in Linux, Docker, Cloud, and DevOps technologies to move toward SRE/DevOps roles. I’m looking for opportunities with broader technical exposure, automation practices, and long-term growth.”

2. Why are you changing jobs?

“I’ve gained valuable experience in production support and banking operations. Now I’m looking for opportunities that provide more exposure to cloud technologies, automation, infrastructure management, and DevOps practices aligned with my long-term career goals.”

3. Explain current responsibilities

Keep concise:

“My responsibilities include:

Monitoring and supporting production applications
Incident management and RCA coordination
Deployment and release activities
Troubleshooting production issues
Server/application health checks
Coordination with internal teams/vendors
Handling change activities and maintenance windows
Supporting critical banking transactions and ensuring service availability
Team coordination and operational ownership”


4. Why should we hire you?

“I bring 6 years of hands-on experience in production environments, incident handling, deployment support, and managing critical issues in the banking domain. I’m comfortable working under pressure and taking ownership. Along with operational experience, I’m strengthening skills in Linux, Docker, Cloud, and DevOps, which helps me contribute toward SRE-oriented responsibilities.”


5. Strength

“My strengths are ownership during critical incidents, troubleshooting, and coordinating across teams to resolve issues quickly while maintaining service stability.”


6. Weakness

Use a growth-oriented answer:

“Earlier, my exposure was primarily focused on support operations. To expand my technical capability, I’ve been actively learning Cloud and DevOps technologies.”

7. Current CTC / Expected CTC

Current:

“My current CTC is ₹12.65 LPA.”

Expected (reasonable target):

For SRE/DevOps transition:

“Based on my experience and responsibilities, I’m expecting around ₹16–18 LPA. However, I’m open to discussion depending on role scope and growth opportunities.”

8. Notice period

“My official notice period is 60 days. I’ll check whether an early release is possible depending on project requirements.”

9. HR may ask: “Do you have DevOps experience?”

Don’t say “No.”

Say:

“My primary experience is in production support, deployments, Linux-based operations, and troubleshooting. Alongside this, I’m actively building hands-on knowledge in Docker, Cloud, and DevOps tools to transition toward SRE/DevOps responsibilities.”
